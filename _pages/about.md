## Computational models of language learning and processing

I study how humans and language models learn, process, and generalize language.

My work connects cognitive science, second language acquisition, and machine learning to build human-grounded models of language behavior and better ways to evaluate language technology.

## Selected Work

### [Tracing L1 interference in English learner writing](/projects/#tracing-l1-interference-in-english-learner-writing)
Longitudinal learner corpus design and annotation for studying how native language shapes learner English, and for evaluating whether models capture transfer effects in principled ways.

### [Controlled generation for language pedagogy](/projects/#controlled-generation-for-language-pedagogy)
Constraint-based text generation for instructional goals, with evaluation centered on linguistic correctness and pedagogical fit.

### [Human-grounded modeling across languages](/research/)
Computational predictors of learning and processing linked to behavioral data across languages.

### [Production-grade data systems](/projects/#large-scale-data-systems-and-infrastructure)
Large-scale engineering experience building and maintaining data platforms, distributed pipelines, and real-time systems in healthcare and industrial settings.

## Collaboration

I am interested in collaborations on learner language, multilingual NLP, model evaluation, and human-grounded language technology.
